What is The Oscars?

The Academy Awards, popularly known as the Oscars, are awards for artistic and technical merit in the film industry. It is regarded as the most famous and prestigious awards in the entertainment industry. The Academy Awards ceremony was first broadcast by radio in 1930 and was televised for the first time in 1953.
